Stealer Logs

View information about your data that has been found in malware stealer logs.

What are stealer logs? Stealer logs are created by malware running on infected machines that creates collections of email addresses, passwords and the websites they were entered into. When stealer logs are added to HIBP, the domain of each website a victim's email address appears against is also loaded.

The list below shows each entry against your email address or in other words, each website the malware watched you enter your email address and password into.

DNS TXT Record Verification

Add the following TXT record to your domain's DNS settings:

hibp-verify=abc123def456

DNS changes may take up to 24 hours to propagate. Once you've added the record, click "Verify Now".

File Upload Verification

Upload the following file to your website's root directory:

Filename: hibp-verification.txt

Content: hibp-verify=abc123def456

The file should be accessible at: https://yourdomain.com/hibp-verification.txt

Meta Tag Verification

Add the following meta tag to the <head> section of your website's homepage:

<meta name="hibp-verification" content="abc123def456">

Once you've added the meta tag, click "Verify Now".
